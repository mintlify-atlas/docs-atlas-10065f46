Swagger Core is a Java library that reads your JAX-RS resource classes and annotations to produce an OpenAPI 3.x specification document automatically. It supports both `javax` and `jakarta` namespaces, OpenAPI 3.0 and 3.1, and integrates with Maven, Gradle, and servlet containers.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add the dependency">
    Include `swagger-jaxrs2` (or its Jakarta counterpart) in your Maven or Gradle build.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Annotate your resources">
    Use `@Operation`, `@Parameter`, `@Schema`, and other OpenAPI annotations on your JAX-RS resource classes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure the context">
    Register the `OpenApiResource` endpoint or use the Maven/Gradle plugin to resolve and output the spec.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Serve or export the spec">
    Access the live OpenAPI JSON/YAML at `/openapi.json` or `/openapi.yaml`, or write it to a file at build time.
  </Step>
</Steps>
